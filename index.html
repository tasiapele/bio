    About Me

    I am from Oceanside, CA and grew up as an only child. I am half Samoan, half white, and feel that I can bring fresh ideas to the table when it includes other cultures, because of my multi-diversity. I moved to Boise after high school to attend Boise State University, and spent two years there studying art education. I moved to Cheney, WA to attend EWU in the Fall of 2012 to pursue a degree in graphic design. Today I am interested in a fast-paced design job that continues to challenge me and allows me to meet amazing people with passion.
